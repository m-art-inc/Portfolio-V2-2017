About

Martin is a versatile artist and designer and a recent college graduate. He attended Portland State University earning a BA in Art Practices and a minor in Graphic Design. He enjoys working on different types of projects such as acrylic paintings, drawings, illustrations and shooting some photography. He understands the importance of maintaining a balance between the analog and digital world. Therefore, he also enjoys working in computer-based projects involving typography, color theory, web, graphic, UI, icon and print design.

By nature, he’s a problem solver and someone that likes to think like an engineer. Martin has good organizational skills and high attention to details and loves to collaborate with other designers. He’s a hard working and self-motivated individual who will always maintain a positive attitude under any circumstance. He is proactive in learning new skills and learning about new technologies.

Skills

Some of my skills include Photoshop, Illustratot, InDesign and several other programs.
